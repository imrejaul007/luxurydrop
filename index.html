About Us

Building Tomorrow's Luxury Today

We are committed to delivering exceptional products and experiences that define standard of luxury in our industry.

A Visionary Approach to Luxury

At Luxury Drop L.L.C, we believe that true luxury lies in intersection of quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. Founded in Dubai in 2020, we have rapidly established ourselves as a leader in premium beauty and personal care sector.

Our diverse portfolio spans three specialized brands, each operating independently while benefiting from our collective expertise, resources, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

The story of our growth, innovation, and commitment to excellence.

2020

Foundation

Luxury Drop L.L.C was established in Dubai with a vision to bring premium beauty and personal care products to Middle East market.

2021

First Brand Launch

Thick Fiber was launched, introducing revolutionary hair building solutions to customers across UAE.

2022

Expansion

Perfumy was launched, expanding our portfolio into designer-inspired fragrances and perfumes.

2023

Premium Addition

Luxury Oudh joined our portfolio, bringing authentic Oudh products to connoisseurs across region.

2025

Global Expansion

Thick Fiber expanded globally to USA, UK, Canada, India, Australia, and Saudi Arabia. Now serving over 50,000 customers worldwide with our premium product range.
